Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W Project

by Reece Clem, Nasir Mendez, and Marquise Allsup

We are students at Frostburg State University in Computer Science

We made a project using a Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W and a US-100 (Ultrasonic) sensor

The Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W is a compact and affordable computer with built-in wireless connectivity, suitable for IoT and embedded projects. This project leverages its efficiency for real-time ultrasonic distance measurement.

The BatBox is a 3D-printed housing designed to protect the Raspberry Pi and ultrasonic sensor while ensuring accurate distance measurements. Its compact design makes it ideal for outdoor or portable applications.
